Dr. Joongheon Kim has been with Korea University (School of Electrical Engineering), Seoul, Republic of Korea, as an assistant professor, since September 2019. He received his B.S. (2004) and M.S. (2006) in Computer Science and Engineering from Korea University, Seoul, Republic of Korea; and his Ph.D. (2014) in Computer Science from the University of Southern California (USC), Los Angeles, California, USA, with two additional masters in Electrical Engineering and Computer Science (Specialization in High Performance Computing and Simulations). He was with LG Electronics - Seocho R&D Campus as a research engineer (Seoul, Korea, 2006--2009), InterDigital as an intern (San Diego, California, USA, 2012), Intel Corporation as a systems engineer (Santa Clara in Silicon Valley, California, USA, 2013--2016), and Chung-Ang University - College of Computer Science and Software as an assistant professor (Seoul, Korea, 2016--2019). He is a senior member of the IEEE (since 2018), and a member of the ACM (since June 2014), IEEE Communications Society, IEEE Computer Society, and IEEE Vehicular Technology Society. He was a recipient of LG Electronics 2007 Outstanding Research Paper Award (2008), USC Annenberg Graduate Fellowship Award with his Ph.D. admission from USC (2009), Intel Corporation Next Generation and Standards (NGS) Division Recognition Award (2015), and Haedong Young Scholar Award (2018).
